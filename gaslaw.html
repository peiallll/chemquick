The Ideal Gas Law is a fundamental equation in chemistry, describing how gases behave under a wide range of conditions, given by the formula PV=nRT. It shows a predictable relationship between Pressure, Volume, Temperature and amount. Changing one of the variables causes the rest to change to adapt to the conditions.
