A Mole represents 6.022*10^23 atoms. We are able to use this number to calculate the number of atoms in a given substance, given mass and formula mass. 1 mole of carbon-12 weights exactly 12 grams, and we are able to calculate the Mole number (Avogadro's Number) based off of this fact.
